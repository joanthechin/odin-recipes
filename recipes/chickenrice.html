Chicken Rice

A delicious plate of chicken rice hehe

Chicken rice is a delicious and comforting dish that originates from various culinary traditions around the world, including Hainanese chicken rice, a popular Singaporean and Malaysian version. This dish is a harmonious blend of tender chicken, fragrant rice, and an array of flavorful condiments, resulting in a satisfying and well-balanced meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (about 3-4 pounds)
  • 2-3 slices of ginger
  • 4-5 cloves of garlic
  • Salt
  • oil (optional)

Recipe

  1. Poach a whole chicken in ginger and garlic-infused water until cooked.
  2. Cook fragrant rice in the chicken broth with ginger and garlic.
  3. Prepare chili sauce and ginger sauce.
  4. Serve the chicken and rice with the sauces, cucumber slices, and cilantro garnish.
