Singapore Laksa

Laksa is a rich and aromatic Southeast Asian soup known for its creamy coconut milk broth, spicy flavors, and a variety of noodles, often accompanied by ingredients like shrimp, chicken, tofu, and herbs.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Coconut milk
  • Laksa paste
  • Rice noodles
  • Fresh herbs and bean sprouts

Recipe

  1. Cook chicken: Poach or pan-fry chicken until cooked through. Slice into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Make laksa broth: Simmer laksa paste with coconut milk and water until well combined and aromatic.
  3. Cook noodles: Boil rice noodles until tender, then drain.
  4. Assemble: Place noodles in a bowl, top with chicken, and pour hot laksa broth over them. Garnish with fresh herbs and bean sprouts. Serve hot.
